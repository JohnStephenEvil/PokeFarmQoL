Reset

Want to reset your Dex progress to enjoy filling it again? Well, you can!

The BFM is a very large magnet that is capable of wiping your Dex progress. When you activate it, you can choose to reset only your EggDex and Pokédex, or you can go more powerful and wipe ShinyDex, AlbinoDex and MelanDex too. It will take 48 hours to charge, giving you plenty of time if you change your mind.
