About the DayCare

The DayCare is a place where you may leave Pokémon to be raised in the care of Professor Laurel. With the assistance of his Grotle, he will help your Pokémon grow steadily.

You may leave up to two Pokémon here at a time. If the two Pokémon are compatible for breeding, they may appear with Eggs!

If this happens, Prof. Laurel will hold them for you. You may adopt up to 6 per day from the DayCare. Any Eggs left here will be released to the Shelter after 24 hours for someone else to adopt.

You have Hypermode, which doubles your daily adoption allowance. You may adopt up to 12 Eggs per day.

Upgrades

The SpyGlass is a simple lens that allows you to identify the appearance of Eggs before you adopt them. For your convenience, Eggs will be grouped based on their appearance. This is most useful when breeding between two species, or when a species has a parallel (such as Miltank and Tauros, they can breed each other's Eggs), but especially when attempting to breed Exclusives. Without the SpyGlass, you'd never know what you've bred!

You already have a SpyGlass. You can buy another to trade it, but it won't give you any further bonuses.

Price: 20 18 

The Matchup Checker is an item that allows you to see exactly how compatible a breeding pair is. This will be the base chance for producing Eggs, although there are hidden modifiers that may mean you get Eggs more or less often than the base compatibility suggests. The compatibility shown should be considered a guideline amount, given suitable conditions, that a couple will breed.

You already have a Matchup Checker. You can buy another to trade it, but it won't give you any further bonuses.

Price: 100 90 

Each DayCare EXP Booster increases the rate at which your Pokémon gain EXP in the DayCare by 25%. More accurately, it decreases the delay in getting each point. This effect stacks up to 10 times.

You have 10 DayCare EXP Boosters. Your Pokémon will gain 1 EXP every 3.87 seconds.

You have reached the limit of this item's effect. You can buy another to trade it, but it won't give you any further bonuses.

Price: 300 270 

Normally, you can only adopt a limited number Egg from the DayCare each day. However, in exchange for a DayCare Pass, you may adopt an extra Egg. Each pass is valid for a single adoption, and is taken away when used.

You have 30 DayCare Passes.

The Everstone is typically used to prevent a Pokémon's evolution, but it serves an additional purpose: give it to a breeding parent and it will receive a 50% chance of passing its Nature to its child.

You have 4 Everstones in your inventory.

Price: 500 × 

Advanced breeders may be aware that Pokémon hatched from Eggs inherit some of their IVs from their parents. This allows you to breed stronger Pokémon! Normally, 3 IVs are passed down at random.

The Destiny Knot can be given to either parent to increase the number of passed IVs from 3 to 5, making it much easier to breed down the stats you want.

Price: 1,000 900 

The Red Gigaremo is a machine that produces artificial bonds in Pokémon and can be used to double the rate at which your Pokémon breed. However, using this machine makes the Pokémon unhappy. Low Happiness may affect the Pokémon's willingness to breed. Use this item with caution!

Price: 2,000 1,800
